Mobile Advertising Services Privacy Policy

1. How We Collect and Use your Information

If you are a consumer that downloads and uses an application which contains the MobGi SDK or otherwise interacts with the Services, we may collect certain device information including the following:

  • Device make, model and operating system;
  • Device properties related to screen size & orientation, audio volume and battery, device memory usage;
  • Carrier;
  • Name and properties of mobile application through which a consumer interacts with the Services;
  • Country, time zone and locale settings (country and preferred language);
  • Network connection type and speed;
  • IP Address;
  • Internet browser user-agent used to access the Services;
  • Advertising (IDFA) or the Google Advertising ID
  • network provider;
  • timestamp;
  • API key (identifier for application);
  • SDK version, application version, OS version of device;
  • network status such as WiFi;
  • the geo-location of your device (using GPS or other geo-location data);
  • Unique device identifier generated from the device MAC/IMEI/MEID, which we alter to limit the ability to identify the relevant device in the future;
  • content type of the ad;
  • what page the ad appeared on;
  • whether you viewed, clicked, or otherwise interacted with the ad, the time of your visit, click, or other interaction or other ad engagement history about your interaction with the ads.

We may use the information collected to evaluate Users' interests and behavior in order to being able to deliver tailored content if necessary, we may use identifiable information to enforce its terms, policies and legal agreements, to comply with court orders and warrants, and assist law enforcement agencies, to collect debts, prevent fraud, misappropriation, infringements, identity thefts and any other misuse of the Mobile Advertising Services, and to take any action in any legal dispute and proceeding.

Where required, our partner will obtain your consent for and on our behalf for the processing of your personal information to serve ads on your device. You can withdraw your consent at any time.

Even if you do not consent to processing of your personal information, we will still process your personal data when necessary to comply with law or to support our legitimate interests. For example, We will collect your device properties related to screen size & orientation, audio volume and battery, device memory usage to optimize the display of ads and avoid crash, We will collect your idfa to distribute ads for you, we will collect your IP address to perform a lookup of your country so that we know whether you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom or Switzerland and also, we will receive and send your IP address to our service providers for purposes of detecting invalid clicks and other fraudulent activity on us.
